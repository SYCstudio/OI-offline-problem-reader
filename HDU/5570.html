HDU5570. balls

内存限制：6000/3000 MS (Java/Others) 时间限制：65536/65536 K (Java/Others)

题目描述

There are $n$ balls with $m$ colors. The possibility of that the color of the $i$-th ball is color $j$ is $\frac{a_{i,j}}{a_{i,1}+a_{i,2}+...+a_{i,m}}$. If the number of balls with the $j$-th is $x$, then you should pay $x^2$ as the cost. Please calculate the expectation of the cost.

输入格式

Several test cases(about $5$)

For each cases, first come 2 integers, $n,m(1 \leq n \leq 1000,1 \leq m \leq 1000)$

Then follows $n$ lines with $m$ numbers $a_{i,j} ( 1 \leq a_i \leq 100)$

输出格式

For each cases, please output the answer with two decimal places.

样例

样例输入

2 2
1 1
3 5

2 2
4 5
4 2

2 2
2 4
1 4

样例输出

3.00
2.96
3.20
