All the Inputs are legal</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Output</div> <div class=panel_content>Output the GPA of each case as discribed above, if the GPA is not existed, ouput:&quot;Sorry!&quot;, else just output the GPA value which is rounded to the 2 digits after the decimal point.
