<tr><td align=center><h1 style='color:#1A5CC8'>The Diophantine Equation</h1><font><b><span style='font-family:Arial;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;color:green'>Time Limit: 1000/500 MS (Java/Others)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Memory Limit: 32768/32768 K (Java/Others)<br>Total Submission(s): 1373&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Accepted Submission(s): 395<br></span></b></font><br><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Problem Description</div> <div class=panel_content>We will consider a linear Diaphonic equation here and you are to find out whether the equation is solvable in non-negative integers or not.<br>Easy, is not it?</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Input</div> <div class=panel_content>There will be multiple cases. Each case will consist of a single line expressing the Diophantine equation we want to solve. The equation will be in the form ax + by = c. Here a and b are two positive integers expressing the co-efficient of two variables x and y. <br>There are spaces between:<br>      1. “ax” and ‘+’<br>      2.  ‘+’ and “by”<br>3.  “by” and ‘=’<br>4. ‘=’ and “c”<br><br>c is another integer that express the result of ax + by. -1000000&lt;c&lt;1000000. All other integers are positive and less than 100000. Note that, if a=1 then ‘ax’ will be represented as ‘x’ and same for by.</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Output</div> <div class=panel_content>You should output a single line containing either the word “Yes.” or “No.” for each input cases resulting either the equation is solvable in non-negative integers or not. An equation is solvable in non-negative integers if for non-negative integer value of x and y the equation is true. There should be a blank line after each test cases. Please have a look at the sample input-output for further clarification.</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Sample Input</div><div class=panel_content><pre><div style="font-family:Courier New,Courier,monospace;">2x + 3y = 10
15x + 35y = 67
x + y = 0</div></pre></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Sample Output</div><div class=panel_content><pre><div style="font-family:Courier New,Courier,monospace;">Yes.

No.

Yes.

HINT: The first equation is true for x = 2, y = 2. So, we get, 2*2 + 3*2=10.
Therefore, the output should be “Yes.”</div></pre></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Author</div> <div class=panel_content>Muhammed Hedayet</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Source</div> <div class=panel_content> <a href="/search.php?field=problem&key=HDOJ+Monthly+Contest+%A8C+2010.01.02&source=1&searchmode=source"> HDOJ Monthly Contest – 2010.01.02 </a> </div> <div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Recommend</div> <div class=panel_content>chenheng</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><center style='font-size:15px;font-family:Arial;font-weight:bold;color:#1A5CC8'><a href='statistic.php?pid=3270'>Statistic</a>&nbsp;|&nbsp;<a href='submit.php?pid=3270'>Submit</a>&nbsp;|&nbsp;<a href="./discuss/problem/list.php?problemid=3270">Discuss</a> | <a href='note/note.php?pid=3270'>Note</a><br></td></tr><tr>
