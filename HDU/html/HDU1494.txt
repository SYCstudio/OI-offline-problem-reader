8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 8 1 1 8 8</div></pre></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Sample Output</div><div class=panel_content><pre><div style="font-family:Courier New,Courier,monospace;">145
