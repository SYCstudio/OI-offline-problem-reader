He also wants to maximize the length of the shortest palindromic string.<br><br>For example, there are 4 kinds of characters denoted as 'a', 'b', 'c', 'd' and the quantity of each character is $\{2,3,2,2\}$ . Professor Zhang can build {&quot;acdbbbdca&quot;}, {&quot;abbba&quot;, &quot;cddc&quot;}, {&quot;aca&quot;, &quot;bbb&quot;, &quot;dcd&quot;}, or {&quot;acdbdca&quot;, &quot;bb&quot;}.
