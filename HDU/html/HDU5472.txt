(Java/Others)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Memory Limit: 65536/65536 K (Java/Others)<br>Total Submission(s): 273&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Accepted Submission(s): 41<br></span></b></font><br><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Problem Description</div> <div class=panel_content>As&nbsp;we&nbsp;all&nbsp;know,&nbsp;a&nbsp;piece&nbsp;of&nbsp;code&nbsp;may&nbsp;include&nbsp;<b>variable</b>,&nbsp;<b>function</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;some&nbsp;other&nbsp;<b>program&nbsp;statements</b>&nbsp;such&nbsp;as&nbsp;<b>if</b>,&nbsp;<b>else</b>,&nbsp;<b>while</b>,&nbsp;<b>for</b>,&nbsp;etc.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>Usually&nbsp;we&nbsp;cannot&nbsp;guarantee&nbsp;our&nbsp;code&nbsp;is&nbsp;absolutely&nbsp;right.&nbsp;When&nbsp;you&nbsp;didn't&nbsp;get&nbsp;the&nbsp;verdict&nbsp;-&nbsp;'Accepted',&nbsp;you&nbsp;would&nbsp;start&nbsp;debugging.&nbsp;So&nbsp;in&nbsp;ACMICPC&nbsp;contests,&nbsp;we&nbsp;have&nbsp;team-mates&nbsp;to&nbsp;help&nbsp;us.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>When you copy a program to windows from linux and open it with notepad, you can see all in one line.<br>Of course, the code can be compiled with g++ on linux, but it's not formatted, so it's too hard to read it.<br>Now you are given a piece of code, and your task is to format the code to make it good.<br><br>To&nbsp;make&nbsp;it&nbsp;simple,&nbsp;we&nbsp;define&nbsp;the&nbsp;code&nbsp;only&nbsp;contain&nbsp;<b>header&nbsp;file</b>,&nbsp;<b>variable</b>,&nbsp;<b>function</b>,&nbsp;<b>if</b>,&nbsp;<b>for</b>,&nbsp;<b>printf</b>,&nbsp;<b>scanf</b>,&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>expression(e.g.&nbsp;a&nbsp;=&nbsp;b&nbsp;+&nbsp;1)</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>Also&nbsp;a&nbsp;piece&nbsp;of&nbsp;code&nbsp;is&nbsp;called&nbsp;good&nbsp;if&nbsp;it&nbsp;obey&nbsp;as&nbsp;follows:<br>1.&nbsp;header&nbsp;file<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A&nbsp;space&nbsp;between&nbsp;<b>#include</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>&lt;XXX&gt;</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;required.&nbsp;And&nbsp;<b>XXX</b>&nbsp;in&nbsp;<b>&lt;&gt;</b>&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;any&nbsp;correct&nbsp;header&nbsp;file.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You&nbsp;can&nbsp;only&nbsp;declare&nbsp;one&nbsp;header&nbsp;file&nbsp;in&nbsp;one&nbsp;line.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;guaranteed&nbsp;that&nbsp;all&nbsp;header&nbsp;files&nbsp;declaration&nbsp;are&nbsp;at&nbsp;the&nbsp;very&nbsp;beginning&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;code.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A&nbsp;blank&nbsp;line&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;after&nbsp;all&nbsp;header&nbsp;files&nbsp;if&nbsp;header&nbsp;files&nbsp;exist.<br>2.&nbsp;variable&nbsp;declaration&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>X&nbsp;A,&nbsp;B,&nbsp;C,&nbsp;...;</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Assuming&nbsp;that&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;only&nbsp;<b>int</b>,&nbsp;<b>double</b>,&nbsp;<b>bool</b>&nbsp;or&nbsp;<b>char</b>, and there must be at least one space after X.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You&nbsp;can&nbsp;only&nbsp;declare&nbsp;one&nbsp;type&nbsp;of&nbsp;variable&nbsp;in&nbsp;one&nbsp;line.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The&nbsp;comma(<b>,</b>)&nbsp;is&nbsp;used&nbsp;to&nbsp;split&nbsp;variables&nbsp;in&nbsp;one&nbsp;type&nbsp;in&nbsp;one&nbsp;line.&nbsp;And&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;after&nbsp;the&nbsp;comma(<b>,</b>).<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;guaranted&nbsp;that&nbsp;there&nbsp;must&nbsp;be&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;between&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>A</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>A</b>,&nbsp;<b>B</b>,&nbsp;<b>C</b>,&nbsp;...,&nbsp;are&nbsp;the&nbsp;name&nbsp;of&nbsp;variable,&nbsp;and&nbsp;guarantee&nbsp;that&nbsp;no&nbsp;calculation,&nbsp;just&nbsp;declaration.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The&nbsp;name&nbsp;of&nbsp;variable&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;any&nbsp;<b>except&nbsp;&quot;scanf&quot;&nbsp;and&nbsp;&quot;printf&quot;</b>.<br>3.&nbsp;function&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>X&nbsp;Y(A&nbsp;a,&nbsp;B&nbsp;b,&nbsp;...){}</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;type&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;function,&nbsp;which&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;4&nbsp;variables&nbsp;above&nbsp;plus&nbsp;<b>void</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>Y</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;name&nbsp;of&nbsp;the&nbsp;function,&nbsp;which&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;any&nbsp;<b>except&nbsp;&quot;scanf&quot;&nbsp;and&nbsp;&quot;printf&quot;</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>A</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>B</b>&nbsp;the&nbsp;type&nbsp;of&nbsp;variable&nbsp;<b>a</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>b</b>,&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>a</b>,&nbsp;<b>b</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;name&nbsp;which&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;any.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;A&nbsp;space&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;between&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>Y</b>,&nbsp;<b>A</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>a</b>,&nbsp;<b>B</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>b</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>...</b>&nbsp;means&nbsp;if&nbsp;the&nbsp;function&nbsp;have&nbsp;more&nbsp;parameters,&nbsp;the&nbsp;format&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;same&nbsp;as&nbsp;above.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;any&nbsp;parameters(0&nbsp;included)&nbsp;in&nbsp;each&nbsp;function.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;It&nbsp;is&nbsp;guaranteed&nbsp;that&nbsp;there&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;between&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>Y</b>.<br>A space is required after each &quot;,&quot;.<br>4.&nbsp;if&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>if(X){}</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;an&nbsp;expression<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There&nbsp;must&nbsp;be&nbsp;<b>{</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>}</b>&nbsp;after&nbsp;<b>if(X)</b>.<br>5.&nbsp;for&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>for(X;&nbsp;Y;&nbsp;Z){}</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>X</b>,&nbsp;<b>Y</b>,&nbsp;<b>Z</b>&nbsp;are&nbsp;three&nbsp;expressions&nbsp;split&nbsp;by&nbsp;<b>;</b>,&nbsp;and&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;after&nbsp;each&nbsp;<b>;</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There&nbsp;must&nbsp;be&nbsp;<b>{</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>}</b>&nbsp;after&nbsp;<b>for(X;&nbsp;Y;&nbsp;Z)</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>X</b>, <b>Y</b>, <b>Z</b> can be empty (e.g. <b>for(; ; )</b>, <b>for(X; ; Z)</b>, <b>for(; Y; )</b>, etc)<br>6.&nbsp;printf&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>printf(&quot;X&quot;,&nbsp;Y,&nbsp;Z,&nbsp;...);</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>X</b>&nbsp;in&nbsp;<b>&quot;&quot;</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;a&nbsp;string&nbsp;which&nbsp;you&nbsp;shouldn't&nbsp;change&nbsp;it.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>Y,&nbsp;Z,&nbsp;...</b>&nbsp;are&nbsp;expressions&nbsp;split&nbsp;by&nbsp;<b>,</b>,&nbsp;and&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;after&nbsp;each&nbsp;<b>&quot;,&quot;</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;The&nbsp;number&nbsp;of&nbsp;expressions&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;0&nbsp;or&nbsp;more.<br>7.&nbsp;scanf&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>scanf(&quot;X&quot;,&nbsp;&amp;Y,&nbsp;Z,&nbsp;...);</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Abosolutely&nbsp;the&nbsp;same&nbsp;with&nbsp;<b>printf</b><br>8.&nbsp;expression&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;There&nbsp;could&nbsp;be&nbsp;<b>variables</b>,&nbsp;<b>operator</b>(<b>+</b>,&nbsp;<b>-</b>,&nbsp;<b>*</b>,&nbsp;<b>/</b>,&nbsp;<b>=</b>,&nbsp;<b>&lt;</b>,&nbsp;<b>&gt;</b>,&nbsp;<b>&gt;=</b>,&nbsp;<b>&lt;=</b>,&nbsp;<b>==</b>,&nbsp;<b>!=</b>)&nbsp;and&nbsp;some&nbsp;<b>constant</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;For&nbsp;each&nbsp;<b>operator</b>,&nbsp;a&nbsp;space&nbsp;is&nbsp;required&nbsp;for&nbsp;both&nbsp;sides.<br>9.&nbsp;function<br><b>X(A,&nbsp;B,&nbsp;C,&nbsp;...);</b><br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;You&nbsp;can&nbsp;call&nbsp;a&nbsp;function&nbsp;after&nbsp;your&nbsp;declaration.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Especially,&nbsp;whatever&nbsp;the&nbsp;type&nbsp;your&nbsp;function&nbsp;is,&nbsp;there&nbsp;will&nbsp;be&nbsp;no&nbsp;<b>return</b>&nbsp;in&nbsp;function's&nbsp;<b>{}</b>.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;<b>A,&nbsp;B,&nbsp;C,&nbsp;...</b>&nbsp;are&nbsp;expressions.<br>&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Function&nbsp;call&nbsp;cannot&nbsp;appear&nbsp;in&nbsp;expressions.&nbsp;Although&nbsp;this&nbsp;may&nbsp;be&nbsp;rediculous,&nbsp;it's&nbsp;much&nbsp;more&nbsp;easier&nbsp;for&nbsp;your&nbsp;coding.<br>A space is required after each &quot;,&quot;.<br><br>In&nbsp;addition,&nbsp;there&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;many&nbsp;<b>expressions</b>,&nbsp;<b>scanf</b>,&nbsp;<b>print</b>,&nbsp;<b>variable&nbsp;declaration</b>&nbsp;inside&nbsp;<b>{}</b>&nbsp;&nbsp;<br><b>{</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;<b>}</b>&nbsp;should&nbsp;both&nbsp;take&nbsp;up&nbsp;one&nbsp;line.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>The&nbsp;rule&nbsp;inside&nbsp;<b>{}</b>&nbsp;is&nbsp;the&nbsp;same,&nbsp;but&nbsp;should&nbsp;start&nbsp;with&nbsp;two&nbsp;more&nbsp;space.<br><br>In&nbsp;all,&nbsp;all&nbsp;characters&nbsp;appear&nbsp;in&nbsp;your&nbsp;code&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;only&nbsp;<b>[a-z][A-Z][0-9]&lt;&gt;()\+-*/=!,;.%&amp;#&quot;'</b>&nbsp;and&nbsp;space.<br><br>The&nbsp;sample&nbsp;involved&nbsp;all&nbsp;rules&nbsp;above.&nbsp;Just&nbsp;have&nbsp;a&nbsp;look&nbsp;at&nbsp;it&nbsp;to&nbsp;unstander&nbsp;better.<br><br>It&nbsp;is&nbsp;guaranteed&nbsp;that&nbsp;the&nbsp;code&nbsp;after&nbsp;formatting&nbsp;can&nbsp;be&nbsp;compiled&nbsp;by&nbsp;g++,&nbsp;so&nbsp;your&nbsp;task&nbsp;is&nbsp;only&nbsp;to&nbsp;format&nbsp;it!</div><div
