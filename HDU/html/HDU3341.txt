They always play &quot;number game&quot;(A boring game based on number theory) together. We all know that AekdyCoin is the man called &quot;nuclear weapon of FZU,descendant of Jingrun&quot;, because of his talent in the field of number theory. So Lost had never won the game. He was so ashamed and angry, but he didn't know how to improve his level of number theory.<br><br>One noon, when Lost was lying on the bed, the Spring Brother poster on the wall(Lost is a believer of Spring Brother) said hello to him! Spring Brother said, &quot;I'm Spring Brother, and I saw AekdyCoin shames you again and again. I can't bear my believers were being bullied. Now, I give you a chance to rearrange your gene sequences to defeat AekdyCoin!&quot;.<br><br>It's soooo crazy and unbelievable to rearrange the gene sequences, but Lost has no choice. He knows some genes called &quot;number theory gene&quot; will affect one &quot;level of number theory&quot;. And two of the same kind of gene in different position in the gene sequences will affect two &quot;level of number theory&quot;, even though they overlap each other. There is nothing but revenge in his mind. AA
AAA
0</div></pre></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Sample Output</div><div class=panel_content><pre><div style="font-family:Courier New,Courier,monospace;">Case 1: 3
