<tr><td align=center><h1 style='color:#1A5CC8'>TimeLimit</h1><font><b><span style='font-family:Arial;font-size:12px;font-weight:bold;color:green'>Time Limit: 2000/1000 MS (Java/Others)&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Memory Limit: 32768/32768 K (Java/Others)<br>Total Submission(s): 360&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;Accepted Submission(s): 90<br></span></b></font><br><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Problem Description</div> <div class=panel_content>Magina is surround by hundreds of monsters. Can he survive in T unit times?<br>Now matter Magina or monster have three attributes(ATI, DEF, HP), but Magina have another attributes (MaxHP). Magina can own some equipments as a hero , there are six kinds of equipment <b>(&quot;Head&quot;, &quot;Weapon&quot;, &quot;Armor&quot;,&quot;Shoes&quot;, &quot;Trump&quot;,&quot;Cuff&quot;)</b> in this world. Equipment have three attributes (A, D, MH), each standing the value can increase to ATI, DEF and MaxHP of Magina.<br>To every some kinds of equipment, Magina can only choose one to use. So when he have some kinds of equipments, he will choose the one have the bigger value. The value of equipments define as<b> A*3 + D*2 + MH</b>. If the value of equipments is equal to the one Magina have, he will not choose to change his equipment.<br><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-1.jpg><br><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-2.jpg><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-3.jpg><br><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-4.jpg><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-5.jpg><br><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-6.jpg><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../data/images/2678-7.jpg><br>Now describe a fighting between A and B.<br>We know A hurt B first, the value of harm define as P. <br><b>P = MAX(1,(int)((A's ATI - B's DEF) / ( abs(Xa-Xb) + abs(Ya-Yb) + 1))). </b> (Xa,Ya) and (Xb,Yb) is the position of A and B. So B's HP will reduce P.<br>Than B hurt A. A will loss  MAX(1,(int)((B's ATI - A's DEF) / ( abs(Xa-Xb) + abs(Ya-Yb) + 1))).<br>Every one will die if his HP is small or equal to 0.<br><br>In the 2d-world. Magina standing in the position (0,0) and never moving, in T unit time, n monsters will move nearly to attack he. To every unit time we divide it to four processes.<br>First Magina choose K monsters to attack once each continuously .(He will attack all if there are no K monsters survived ) If a monster die by attacking, he will drop a equipment. Than Magina can choose weather to use it. The way Magina choose is according to the P which is the value of his hurting to the monster. The first K higher monsters will be selected at the every unit time's begining, if two monsters have some P, the smaller ID of monsters will be selected. <br>Second every monster survived will hurt Magina once continuously. If Magina die, game over. <br><br>Third every monster survived will move to Magina one unit. If the monster is standing the place of Magina, he will not move. One unit is meaning X or Y to change only one.<br><br>Fourth Magina will revert W HP. <b>W = (int) ( MaxHP*0.05 ).</b> The maximum of HP can not bigger than MaxHP.<br></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Input</div> <div class=panel_content>The input contains multiple test cases.<br>First line give the integer n (1&lt;= n &lt;=400)<br>Next line ATI, DEF, MaxHP,K,T. (T&lt;=100). Magina is HP = MaxHP and have no equipment at the begin.<br>Next follow n line, each line expressing a monster by increasint ID.The input form as ATI, DEF,HP,X,Y, Kind'smane, A, D, MH. All integer are nonnegative.Last four expressing the equipment this monster owned.Kind'smane is the kind name of equipment.It can only be one of six describing foregoing<br></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Output</div> <div class=panel_content>Output the information of Magina's equipment during the T unit times.<br>If Magina use a equipment when he have no this kind of equipment, Output <b>“Magina get the Sname”</b>.<br>If Magina use a new well equipment, output<b>&quot;Magina update the Sname&quot;</b>, Sname is the kind name of six equipments. <br>Last Output <b>&quot;Magina death !&quot;</b> if Magina is death .Otherwise output <b>&quot;Magina killed Num Monster!&quot;</b>,Num is a numbers of monsters Magina killed during T unit times. <br>Every case follow a blank.<br></div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Sample Input</div><div class=panel_content><pre><div style="font-family:Courier New,Courier,monospace;">1
