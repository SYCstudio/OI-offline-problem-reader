People all bear to watch as this planet on which they have lived for millions of years. Nonetheless, scientists are worrying about anther problem…<br>As we know that long distance space travels are realized through the wormholes, which are given birth by the distortion of the energy fields in space. Airships will be driven into the wormholes to reach the other side of the universe by the suction devices placed in advance. Each wormhole has its configured attract parameters, X, Y or Z. When the value of ID%X is in [Y,Z], this spaceship will be sucked into the wormhole by the huge attraction.
