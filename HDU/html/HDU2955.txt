He has decided to work in the lucrative business of bank robbery only for a short while, before retiring to a comfortable job at a university.<br><br><center><img style='max-width:100%;' src=../../../data/images/con211-1010-1.jpg></center> <br>For a few months now, Roy has been assessing the security of various banks and the amount of cash they hold. She feels that he is safe enough if the banks he robs together give a probability less than this.</div><div class=panel_bottom>&nbsp;</div><br><div class=panel_title align=left>Input</div> <div class=panel_content>The first line of input gives T, the number of cases.
