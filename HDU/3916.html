Hint
The first case can be decomposed to G(1,5)+G(1,6)+G(2,5)+G(3,3), the minimum length is 1 and it can also be decomposed to G(1,3)+G(1,5)+G(2,6)+G(3,5), the minimum length is 3, so answer is 3.
